My Projects

LOS Analysis of Balkhu Staggered Intersection

This project applied Python-based automation, SIDRA modeling, and GIS mapping to analyze and optimize the Balkhu Staggered Intersection, reducing delays by 22% and supporting data-driven traffic management strategies.

Crash Analysis and Hot-spot Identification on Koteswor-Jadibuti Road Section

AThis project applied Python-based automation, GIS mapping, and crash data processing to analyze accident trends on the Koteswor-Jadibuti road section, enabling data-driven road safety improvements.

Detailed Engineering Survey, Design, and Cost Estimation of Chhaling Road

This project combined high-precision surveying, geometric design, 3D modeling, and operational analysis to develop a context-sensitive and cost-effective roadway design for Chhaling Road in compliance with AASHTO and HCM standards.

Metro System Feasibility Study in Kathmandu Valley

This project analyzed the feasibility of a 77.28 km metro system in Kathmandu Valley, addressing cost, ridership, infrastructure challenges, and stakeholder coordination to propose a sustainable and phased development strategy.
